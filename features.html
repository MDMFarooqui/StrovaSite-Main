All features

Everything you need for professional 2D animation, nothing you don't.

Timeline system

Multi-track timeline

Flow, Audio, Keyframe clips on separate tracks. Drag to move, trim handles, magnetic snap.

Timeline zoom

Mouse wheel zoom from 1 frame to full project. Scrub with timeline ruler.

Clip snapping

Magnetic snapping to frames, beats, other clips. Hold Alt for free move.

Scene canvas

Layers + frames

Unlimited layers per scene. Onion skinning shows 2 previous/next frames.

Transform tools

Move, rotate, scale with on-canvas handles. Free transform mode.

Camera controls

Pan, zoom, rotate camera per scene. Keyframe camera moves.

Eraser

Hard/soft edges. Pressure sensitive.

Export & sharing

Video export

GIF export

PNG sequence

Transform keyframes
