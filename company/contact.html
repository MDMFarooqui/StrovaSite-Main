Before you email

Strova crashes on startup

Check the devlog for known issues. If it's not there, open a GitHub issue with your OS and version.

Feature request

We track these on GitHub. Search existing issues first, then open a new one explaining the use case. "Add this because Procreate has it" is not a use case.

Can I invest / partner / acquire?

No. We don't take money. We don't do partnerships. We don't want to be acquired. Don't email about this.

Press inquiry

Email Strovadev@gmail.com with "PRESS:" in the subject. Include your publication and deadline. We'll prioritize if it's real.
