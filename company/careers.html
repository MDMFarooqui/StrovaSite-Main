Join the chaos

We don't have job postings. We have problems that need solving and room for anyone who wants to help.

Strova is built by three teenagers who bit off more than they can chew—in a good way. We need help. Not employees. Not contractors. Just people who give a shit about animation software and want to make something better exist.

No resumes. No interviews. No HR department. Show us what you can do, and you're in.

💻

Show, don't tell

How to actually join

  • Find something broken or missing. Check our GitHub issues, try the alpha, or read the roadmap. Find a problem that annoys you.
  • Fix it or propose a fix. Code a solution, design a mockup, write a draft, or document the bug properly. Do the work first.
  • Show us. Open a pull request, post in Discord, or email a link. We review everything. If it's good, we merge it.
  • Keep doing that. There's no "you're hired" moment. Just keep contributing. Eventually we add you to the team chat and you become part of the decision-making.

📚

🤝

🎓

⏰

No pay (we're broke), but full credit

What we don't want

People looking to pad their resume. People who want to "manage" but not build. People who need constant validation. People who treat open source like free labor for their portfolio.

We want people who use Strova, find it frustrating, and decide to make it less frustrating. People who stick around after their first commit. People who argue with us when we're wrong.

Still interested?

Don't send us a cover letter. Just go to GitHub, find an issue you can solve, and solve it. Or join Discord and ask where help is needed.
